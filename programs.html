Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our programs

Who is eligible for Empower NeXT programs?

Our programs are open to South African youth aged 15-25. We welcome participants from all backgrounds and educational levels. Some programs may have specific eligibility requirements which will be detailed during the application process.

How much do the programs cost?

All Empower NeXT programs are completely free for participants. We believe financial barriers should never prevent young people from accessing opportunities for growth and development.

How long do the programs run?

Program durations vary: Mentorship (6-12 months), Life Skills (8 weeks), Career Preparation (12 weeks), Youth Camps (weekend events), Academic Support (ongoing), and Entrepreneurship (16 weeks).
