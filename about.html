About Empower NeXT

Learn about our mission to empower South Africa's youth

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Empower NeXT emerged from a simple but powerful observation: too many talented young South Africans lack access to opportunities, mentorship, and guidance needed to reach their full potential.

We started as a small community initiative in Johannesburg and have grown to impact hundreds of young lives across multiple provinces.

Our approach combines personalized mentorship with practical skills development, creating a holistic support system for youth aged 15-25.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide South Africa's youth with the mentorship, skills, and opportunities needed to overcome barriers and achieve personal and professional success.

Vision: A South Africa where every young person has access to guidance, support, and opportunities to build a bright future, regardless of their background or circumstances.

We believe that with the right support system, every young person can rise above their challenges and build a meaningful future.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Compassion

We approach every young person with empathy, understanding, and genuine care for their wellbeing and success.

Commitment

We stay dedicated to our youth through challenges and celebrate their successes as our own.

Community

We believe in the power of collective support and building strong networks for our youth.
